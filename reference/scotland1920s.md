# Networks of interlocking directorates

Five affiliation networks constructed from the multiple directors lists
of the top \>100 Scottish companies over five two-year intervals between
1904 and 1974.

## Format

An affiliation network; see
[is_an](http://corybrunson.github.io/bitriad/reference/affiliation_network.md).

## Source

Scott, J., & Hughes, M. (1980). *The Anatomy of Scottish Capital*. Croom
Helm. <http://books.google.com/books?id=59mvAwAAQBAJ>
